CS280 Project - 3D-Reconstruction from Satellite Images

Names: Jeremy Fischer, Jakob Gollreiter, Daniel Won


Abstract

We investigate the feasibility of 3D reconstruction from limited and variable-quality satellite imagery (10-20 views per scene). Through comparison of three approaches - 3D Gaussian Splatting with derived camera parameters, VGGT followed by bundle adjustment, and DUSt3R - we demonstrate that practical 3D reconstruction is achievable despite challenging input conditions. Our evaluation shows VGGT with bundle adjustment produces the most geometrically accurate results, while optimized DUSt3R offers the most reliable reconstructions.
